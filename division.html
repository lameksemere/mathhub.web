Understanding Division

Ages 6–10 · 3 Levels

Division is all about splitting things into equal groups. Imagine you have 12 cookies and want to share them equally among 3 friends. Division helps you figure out exactly how many cookies each friend gets — it's the fair way to share!

12 ÷ 3
Dividend ÷ Divisor
= 4
Quotient
The Opposite Rule
If 3 × 4 = 12
then 12 ÷ 3 = 4 ✓
Zero Rule
You can never divide by zero — it's impossible!

The Opposite Rule

Division is the opposite of multiplication. If 3 × 4 = 12, then 12 ÷ 3 = 4!

block

The Zero Rule

You can NEVER divide any number by zero. It's mathematically impossible!

looks_one

Divide by One

Any number divided by 1 is always itself. 100 ÷ 1 = 100!

tips_and_updates

Use Multiplication!

To check: if you got 12 ÷ 4 = 3, multiply back: 3 × 4 = 12 ✓
