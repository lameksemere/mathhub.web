Understanding Multiplication

Ages 6–10 · 3 Levels

Multiplication is a quick way to do repeated addition. Instead of adding 3 + 3 + 3 + 3, you can just say 3 × 4 = 12. It saves time and makes maths fun!

add

Repeated Addition

12 + 12 + 12 = 36, which is the same as 12 × 3 = 36. Multiplication is the shortcut!

shopping_cart

Real World Magic

4 packs of 12 stickers = 4 × 12 = 48 stickers total. Multiplication saves the day!
